# Ultimate Sentences

My dad’s from Ehime and my mom’s from Hiroshima
so I’ve got roots in the Chugoku–Shikoku area.
My dad worked at an insurance company, and my mom was a stay-at-home mom.  
Because of my dad’s job transfers, I moved around quite a bit.
I was born in Hiroshima,
went to kindergarten in Saitama,  
spent the first three years of elementary school in Shimane,  
the last three years in Osaka,  
junior high and high school in Ehime,  
and university in Fukuoka.
